Tequila Keylime Pie

Chocolate Tequilimepie Version (Blake's Version):
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • 3 cups sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup marscapone cheese
  • 3/4 cup key lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon grated lime zest
  • pinch of vanilla beans, or 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 fl oz of silver or platimun tequila

Directions:

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large bowl or electric mixer combine condensed milk, sour cream and marscapone. Mix at high speed for 2 minutes or whip until you can see small bubbles in the mixture. Add lime juice, lime rind, vanilla bean, and tequila. Mix well and pour into graham cracker crust. Bake in preheated oven for 5 to 8 minutes, until tiny pinhole bubbles burst on the surface of pie. DO NOT BROWN! Chill pie thoroughly before serving. If desired you can add a thin layer of chocolate on top of the crust by mixing an extra half cup of sweetened condensed milk and 1 teaspoon of coaco powder (or more if you like it more bitter) and spreading it evenly on all surfaces of the crust, removing any excess.
